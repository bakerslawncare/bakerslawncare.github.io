About Baker's Lawn Care

Established in 2019, Baker's Lawn Care is a reputable landscaping company based in South China, Maine. Our vision is to be the leading choice of small landscaping businesses by partnering with commercial customers in the Kennebec County area, recognized for the integrity of our workers, ethics of our business practices and quality of our services.

Meet the Owner

Joshua Baker has more than 20 years of landscaping and snow removal experience. His eye for detail is often brought to his attention by his customers and allows us to get the job done right and on time.
